GIS can mean Geographic Information Systems or Geographic Information Science  
it is a term that is used to talk about mapping software and the process of mapping  
GIS is founded in principles of geography which studies the interrelationships of nature  
science people and the world The City of Eugene uses GIS to help us understand our  
community to provide more efficient services in staffing and money and to help us  
prepare for the future It is used in all departments in many forms This can be mapping  
analysis field data collection in brochures reports behind the scenes for data  
analysis and more recently in web applications Explore this webpage to see all  
the different ways staff use mapping to serve our community
